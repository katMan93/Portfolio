Conquer Finals Week with Us!

Free climbing and fitness classes May 1 - 10. Be on the lookout for De-stress fest as well, put on by the Division of Student Affairs. Massages, Coloring Club, and more!

Rock Climbing = Strength + Endurance + Flexibility + Mindfulness = Yoga

Let our Outdoor team lead you through a rock climbing adventure, while our GroupX Instructors lead you through an Yoga warm-up and cool down. This mashup event is free for those that have either a Climbing Wall or GroupX pass. April 20 from 7-9 p.m.

Join us TONIGHT at 6 p.m. at Huntsville Lanes

Register online or on-site for this one-night event. $12 gets you your shoe rental and a chance to be the IM Champ!

Now Hiring Lifeguards for Summer Employment

Looking for a summer job? Join our Aquatics Program. Not Certified? No Problem! We will train those that meet the course prerequisites. For more questions, please inquire at the RSC Pool Office.
